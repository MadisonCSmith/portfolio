Description

This was designed to be more of an infographic than a dashboard. I created this dashboard in reaction to a misleading claim I read about that argued that Seattleâ€™s reputation for rainy weather is a misnomer because other cities get more inches of rain than Seattle. I thought it was important to illustrate that when comparing rain between cities, it is prudent to consider a couple metrics. While the number of inches is a valuable metric, the number of days is also important to consider. One hour of intense heavy rain may get more inches of rain than a day of light drizzle, but I would not consider a city that gets an hour of intense rain to be less rainy than a city that gets light rain all day.

The data used in this dashboard was collected from NOAA.
