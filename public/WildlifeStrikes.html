Description

This dashboard was created to be more of an infographic than a true dashboard. When I first created the dashboard, I was surprised at the number of minor wildlife strikes that happen everyday. I had thought that wildlife strikes were rare incidents that caused critical damage and significant impact to flights. However, this data suggests that wildlife strikes are a much more common occurrence and cause less significant issues than I previously believed. A potential audience for this dashboard is nervous flyers as it gives a good visual of the true risk of wildlife to their flights.

The data used in this dashboard is from a Tableau sample dataset.
