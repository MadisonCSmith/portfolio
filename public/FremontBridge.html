Description

This dashboard was created using data collected by the City of Seattle. This dataset was particularly interesting to me because many people use the Fremont Bridge to commute into downtown Seattle and I was interested in seeing if/how the bike traffic over the bridge changed due to the increase in remote working that occurred during the COVID-19 pandemic. The data shows that the COVID-19 pandemic decreased overall bike traffic over the Fremont Bridge from March, when cases first began to spike in Seattle. The pandemic also changed the patterns of bikes across the bridge, with a slight increase in bikes during the middle of the day and weekends and fewer bikes during rush hours and weekdays.

The data used in this dashboard is from the City of Seattle
